A Short Biography

Steve Jobs, the co-founder of Apple and co-creator of the iPhone had quite a remarkable life. Even though he had dropped out of college, he had an inert intelligence and resourcefullness that conquered technology in ways we never dreamed possible.

Below I highlight the timeline of this great inventor.

  • 1955 - Born in San Francisco, California
  • 1971 - Studied at Stanford University
  • 1972 - Dropped out of Reed College
  • 1974 - Worked as a technician at Atari
  • 1976 - Cofounded Apple with Steve Wozniak
  • 1979 - Funded The Graphics Group - the computer graphics division of LucasFilm
  • 1983 - Developed the Apple Lisa
  • 1984 - Developed the Apple Macintosh
  • 1985 - Jobs was forced out of Apple and founded NEXT
  • 1986 - The Graphics Group becomes PIXAR
  • 1997 - Apple aquired NEXT making Jobs CEO within a few months
  • 2000 - Jobs became permanent CEO
  • 2001 - Jobs was diagnosed with pancreatic cancer
  • 2007 - The first touchscreen cellular smartphone, the iPhone is released
  • 2010 - The iPad is released
  • 2011 - Jobs resigns as CEO due to health complications and makes Tim Cook CEO.
  • 2011 - Steve Jobs passes away from complications related to his pancreatic cancer

For a more complete overview of Steve Jobs' life, please read his wikipedia page.
